Nyonya Makko

This restaurant is quite famous for serving best Nyonya food in Malacca. You can order any type of dish from here. The choices of dishes are Tenggiri Fish in Assam Pedas, Sotong Chili Garam; Beef Rendang, Prawn Stew, Chendol Kacang Dessert, Chicken stew (Ayam Pongteh) which we highly recommend you to try. Most customers here are the locals, because the most of the dishes are Nyonya food. Not many of the Foreigners have tried the food served here. But if they do visit Melacca, we recommend them to dine at this restaurant.

The restaurant is opened during specific hours, like lunch time or dinner time. Customers can only dine during those hours. If they plan to have a big event, they need to make a reservation for it. The food prices are very cheap, you can eat a lot and pay less. The food here are very good, the chicken and potato pieces were well cooked. It takes more time to cook the Prawn Stew and the Beef Rendang, but the Tenggiri Fish in Assam Pedas takes longer time. Because they cook fresh fish which needs to be prepared well for cooking.
