About Us

Foodies House provides informations on good food and their locations in Malaysia. We are a casual guide who are willing to provide food information to all people in details. We visit restaurants, bars and places, trying to separate the good from the bad, the excellent from the extortion. I have to say this, there are lots of good foods from different countries served in Malaysia. Well, we had tried them, would you love to try them ? People search on the internet to look for good food in Malaysia. They might come across our website and find some interest in any of our dishes. And would love to travel with family or friends and try the food together.

Objective

Our main aim is to promote food and culture while providing our viewers with honest reviews.
And keeping in mind that they should be enlightened with the right information.
