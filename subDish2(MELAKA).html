Tandoori Chicken

Tandoori Chicken is known among everyone and is served in almost every Mamak restaurant around Malaysia. We recommend people who are traveling to Malacca or are staying there to try the Tandoori Chicken from this restaurant. This place is always crowded with customers, they are a mix of locals and foreigners. The place has a good view and people can smell the Tandoori Chicken being grilled. Customers can either decide to take a seat in the restaurant or outside. People say that this place is the best in Malacca for serving Tandoori Chicken.

They have a wide range of food choices here, like Naan or Cheese Naan and Dahl to go with the Tandoori Chicken. You can order for Double or Triple Cheese Naan as well which is so soft. Those who love cheese can go with the order and enjoy their meal. The Dahl is great with the meal, the combination of the spice of the dahl with the one of the Tandoori Chicken goes well. They use around 12 types of herbs and spices to marinated their chicken. And the Pudina recipe is prepared with 10 spices and chili padi. The Tandoori Chicken was really good, the naan was well baked and the dahl made the whole dish complete.
