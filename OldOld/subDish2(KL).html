Mutton Masala and Chapati

The Best Mutton Masala in Kuala Lumpur is served in Singh Chapati House. It is near NU Sentral, just 5 minutes walk to the restaurant. The place serves variety of dishes like Chicken Masala, Paneer Masala, plain or butter Chapati, Masala Tea, fresh fruit juice, plain Yogurt drink or fruit Yogurt drink. If you live in Kuala Lumpur and want to try the Masala dishes, we highly recommend you to try this place. It is easy to find and will worth your trip.

It takes a while to cook the dish, you need to wait for the dish to be well cooked and served. Same goes for the bread, they prepare the dough and bake it when costumers orders them. Once you take a seat, the staff will serve you with a glass of water. The mutton masala contains cubes of beef, thick curry which tastes really good. We have been to this place every once in a while because we have immense desire to have another meal. All of the dishes are really good, each time we have been there, we order other dishes to have a great experience of the food and enjoy our time there.
To get to the shop, you have to go to the bus stop at NU Sentral then cross the road. You will find 7 Eleven and KK shop there. You just need to get behind the shops and reach a street, the shop is right next to My Hotel and Restaurant Jaya.
