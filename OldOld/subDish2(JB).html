Chicken Chop

This dish is another local food served at some of the restaurants in Malaysia. Not many Foreigners know about this dish or have tried them. We recommend them to try the best Chicken Chop at the IT Roo Cafe in Johor Bahru. There are two types of chicken chop served at the cafe and they are both Halal for Muslims to eat,they are either grilled or deep fried. It is a good dish for people who are planning to try every different dishes of rice with chicken.

The IT Roo Cafe has been awarded the title of “Best Chicken Chop in Malaysia” by The Star Newspaper in 2003. The place really makes good chicken chop, we would like people try having their first Chicken chop from there. The dish takes lots of time and effort in cooking to make it perfect. To serve the best chicken chop, a thick and juicy chicken thigh needs to be well cooked, it is grilled till perfection and properly added with either black pepper sauce or mushroom sauce. The rice is served hot along with the chicken, and it comes with either potato wedges or coleslaw. The flavor of the sauce is very good with the chicken and rice.
