Laksa Penang

The name Asam Laksa is a delicious noodle dish from Penang at Nan Guang. It is one of the most popular dishes in Malaysia which is known to the locals, most probably after Nasi Lemak and Kuey Teow. It became a famous dish not just in Malaysia, but around South of Asia since when it was first made. Which resulted in cooking variety of Laksa dish in every nations around South of Asia. Whether you are a fan of noodles or not, you will have the immense desire to try another bowl of Laksa. It is hardly resistible after the first try.

The main ingredient is thick rice noodle. It is made with mackerel soup and tamarind fruit, the mixture gives sense to a sour flavor. It does include lemongrass, galangal and chili to give extra boost in its unique flavor. The dish is served fresh and hot, the chicken is well cooked and the soup brings out the best taste out of the dish.
Whenever you want to visit Penang, you must try this awesome dish before leaving. Just ask for a Laksa and you will be given with a bowl of a well-cooked, local Asam Laksa. It is more available in Klang Valley, you can have this delicious dish here as many restaurants serve in Penang Laksa.
