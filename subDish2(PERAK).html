Cheezy Chicken Grilled

Cheezy Chicken Grilled is a delicious large burger that can serve up to 2-4 person. The Burger came new, it is served in Ipoh. Not many parts of Malaysia Fast Food shops serves large burgers like this.

The main feature is the thick large chicken patty. The burger contains lots of fresh lettuce, tomatoes, onions, pickles and with barbeque sauce, mustard and hot sauce. This burger was really good, it was enough for the two of us. We could have ordered fries but we wouldn't be able to finish the burger. If you are planning to hang out with a friend and want to try out a large burger, we would recommend you to go to this place.
